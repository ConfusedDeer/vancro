Services In Support of Global Operations

INTELLIGENCE SUPPORT TO DOD AND THE INTELLIGENCE COMMUNITY

Vancro provides substantive expertise, covering all facets of counterterrorism (CT) analysis and kinetic / non-kinetic targeting support including but not limited to the following: intelligence production tasking; content management and dissemination support; software requirements management; and intelligence systems training activities. Vancro's teams conduct research and analysis of data, produced finished intelligence, populate various databases, and disseminate a broad array of intelligence products dealing with terrorist personalities, facilities, and resources. Vancro personnel provide content management support, software requirements management, intelligence systems training activities, dissemination support, and intelligence production tasking.

CIED OPERATIONS AND EXPLOITATION SUPPORT

IEDs is the main weapon of choice for rogue and insurgent organizations seeking to exert influence by intimidation. Vancro provides Counter IED technical support and expertise by augmenting the warfighter and law enforcement with a variety of services including: Instruction and implementation of incident staff battle drills, Battle Staff IED exploitation synchronization, trends analysis, improvised Explosive Device incident forensic backtracking, intelligence fusion techniques, and staff operations.

UNMANNED AERIAL SYSTEM (UAS) SUPPORT & COUNTER UAS SUPPORT

Vancro continues to provide vital C-UAS support to JIDO and its deployed customers with critical intelligence and operations support. With over 40 operational deployments to the US Central Command area of responsibility (AOR), Vancro continues to provide highly skilled Operations and Intelligence Integrators with C-UAS experience critical to support the war on improvised airborne threats. Our team retains extensive experience in detecting, tracking, fixing and countering UAS activity in deployed areas of operations. We are familiar with emerging UAS tactics, techniques and procedures used by adversaries and we utilize intelligence based methodologies and forensics exploitation to support countering this emerging threat..

SPECIAL OPERATIONS SUPPORT

Vancro provides operations advice to Theater Special Operations Commands (TSOCs) and deployed SOF commanders and staff on all activities concerning planning, and synchronization of effort of deployed assets. Vancro ensures SOF assets are abreast of all current and emerging processes, methodologies, and TTPs for attacking networks that utilize IEDs. We provide expertise in SOF military operations-intelligence fusion and analysis to support deployed U.S. Special Operations Forces in C-IED and Attack the Network (AtN) operations in all geographic Combatant Command Areas of Responsibility.

TRANSLATION AND INTERPRETATION SERVICES SUPPORT

Augmenting Department of Defense Intelligence and Operations services, Vancro specializes in providing Translation and Interpretation services. As a prime contractor, we are supporting the the Department of Veterans Affairs with Language Interpretation to include American Sign Language (ASL) Services for all hearing impaired employees.
